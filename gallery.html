About Us

International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON)

ISKCON Bahrain was started in the year 1981, with a handful of devotees in a small house under the leadership of H.H. Jayapataka Maharaja, a senior disciple of Srila Prabhupada.

It is a spiritual center in the Kingdom of Bahrain where people of all caste, creed or colour can participate in studying and understanding our timeless scriptures, sing, chant and dance in glorifying Lord Sri Krishna. It also conducts many social and cultural programmes that help identify and nurture the talents of individuals in the community to be utilised in the service of God and humanity.
