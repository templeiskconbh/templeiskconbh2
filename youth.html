Caring for our children

Gift our children the treasure of Vedic Wisdomin a fun-filled manner through our Madhav’s Garden Weekend School and Balaramdesh Youth Cluster programs!

Kids

Madhav's Garden

Madhav’s Garden Madhav’s Garden is a weekend school for children between the ages 4 to 14 years from ISKCON Bahrain.

It’s mission is to impart Vedic knowledge to children in a fun-filled and systematic manner.

Your child’s day in Madhav’s Garden will include scriptural studies, arts and crafts, colouring, story time, music and drama.

Kids and Youth

Little Gokul Workshop

Little Gokul Workshop is a annual children’s holiday workshop run by Madhav’s Garden with participation of over 250 children every year. For the first time, it was conducted online in 2020, with participaton of children from across the world.

Youth

Youth Classes

ISKCON Bahrain also conducts special classes for youth, providing much needed spiritual guidance and emotional support at this important juncture of life. It is interactive, driven by the youth and fun-filled with field trips and hands-on activities.
